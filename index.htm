About the Society

The Society was first established in 1980, as a sub-group of the Wellington Postgraduate Medical Society.
Membership of the group is currently free of charge.

All are welcome to attend monthly meetings which are held on the first Wednesday of the months February to December, at 7.30pm,
temporarily in the Newtown Salvation Army Worship and Community Centre in 4 Normanby Street, Newtown.
Attendees should assemble at around 7pm in the foyer.

Offers of presentations for future meetings are always welcome and should be discussed with Jayne Krisjanous, telephone 027 579 8399,
or Michael Harrison, telephone 021 640618.
Presentations are not always of a medical nature but this is the dominant theme.

Anyone interested who is not already on the mailing list should contact
Trevor Williams, phone 04 385 5543, or
Jayne Krisjanous, telephone 027 579 8399.
